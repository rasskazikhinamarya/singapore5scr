"If we can't enjoy our life because of joints, all we can do is waiting for a miracle! " - an interview with the man who performed a miracle!

  Comments 33

Today our guest is the legendary innovator and doctor from Tampines, Angao Goh.

Hello, Dr. Goh. Please, tell us more about yourself.

I was born and raised in Tampines. I graduated from Medical School in 1975. I met my future wife Aria there. Until 2009 I had been working in the specialty, as a podiatrist.

- Interesting. What happened in 2009 and why did you decide to stop working as a podiatrist?

In 2007, Aria was diagnosed with knee osteoarthritis. It is worth mentioning that this was a turning point in our lives, though back then we thought that it was an unbearable challenge. We tried everything in 2 years: physiotherapy, no extra physical activities, vitamin complexes (chondroitin, glucosamine, MSM, etc.), gels, ointments. All for nothing. Nothing worked, we were devastated. I couldn't believe that despite my medical education I couldn't help my wife. I was completely disappointed in our medical system. I started to ask myself: Is there really no way to give a loved one a happy and long life? So I started my research.

- What kind of research?

As soon as you realize that your wife will probably stop walking in a few years, you are willing to do anything. I started my research. I studied all the materials that more or less related to joint diseases. Physiology, psychosomatic symptoms, biochemistry. I spent almost all my money in order to learn from the experience of the best Asian specialists who certainly have a better understanding of joint diseases.

In December 2009, I was done with my research and realized that if you mix certain ingredients, you can make a remedy that will free you from joint pain. Surprisingly, the necessary ingredients weren't available in my country. I borrowed a large amount of money and ordered the ingredients from all over the world. One month later, I received the ingredients, but I got another surprise: no one agreed to produce the product I needed. Fortunately, friends from university helped me. After 3 weeks, I got the formula and Aria tried it.

I was on cloud nine!

- What? What happened?

Aria got better every day. After 7 days of use, she could easily walk to the store, and the smile, that I had missed so much, finally lighted up her face. After two weeks, the arthritis was completely gone! I couldn't believe my eyes, but the analysis showed that she was absolutely healthy. It was a victory!

- It's exactly what everyone dreams about! But you didn't stop there, did you?

Well, at first we just enjoyed our lifes and appreciated every moment. At that time we had a beautiful daughter. This is me with my two beautiful girls.

One evening Aria asked me: How many people are suffering from joint diseases in Singapore? We looked at the figures, and we were shocked. It turned out that more than 1 million people in Singapore have different types of joint diseases and joint pain. She then asked a question that changed my life forever: Can you help the others just like you helped me? Can you make them happy? I fell in love with her all over again. This woman is the best thing that has ever happened to me. I agreed to do so. It took me about three years to refine the formula and create the cream "Pantoflex" - the best product that can afford our people.

- Sounds very encouraging. Tell us more about Pantoflex.

My wife and I did something incredible for the people. I created the formula with a lot of helpful natural ingredients and Aria did the rest of the work, distribution and etc.

Thanks to its unique formula, the Pantoflex cream can help with almost all joint diseases:

- Rheumatic disorder

Don't forget how hard l have worked for that. Soon it will be a year since the production of the cream. After a year, 17,934 people started to enjoy live and forgot about joint pain.
